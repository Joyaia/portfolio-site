Welcome!

I'm Zhuoyao, a junior design student living in New York. Drawing is my way of expressing my passion—bringing my favorite characters and things to life with my brush, weaving them into my own artistic world. I'm passionate about illustration and skilled in digital painting.

This semester, I’ve started exploring animation, which has opened up a whole new creative path for me. Animation not only lets me tell full stories but also gives my artwork life and energy.

Here, you’ll find a mix of my class assignments and personal projects. I hope they give you a glimpse of my passion and creativity. Thanks so much for visiting, and I hope you enjoy my artistic world!
